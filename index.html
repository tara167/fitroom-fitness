01 The idea

Curiosity is
the first rep.

Fit Room was founded by Eileen Ramírez, a Costa Rican who believes movement belongs to everyone. We built a calm, precise space a few steps from the sand — fully-equipped Reformers, master-level teaching, and a method that respects the original Pilates principles.

No gimmicks, no temporary workout high. Just genuine progress in posture, strength and mobility — the kind your body keeps. Come curious. Leave stronger.

“You are only as young as your spine is flexible. Joseph Pilates

Green flags only.
